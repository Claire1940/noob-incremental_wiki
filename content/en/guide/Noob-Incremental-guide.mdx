<Section icon={Trophy} title="Strategic Prestige and Rebuilding">
Prestige is the ultimate progression mechanic in Noob Incremental, designed to unlock new content and provide increasingly powerful resets. Knowing when and how to prestige is key to long-term success.

<Callout type="info">
Prestige should not be rushed. Only reset when the gains from unlocking the next prestige layer significantly outweigh the benefits of continuing your current run. Evaluate your progress carefully.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Your Base Run">
    Start by buying Noobs and generating Oof. Focus on establishing a strong foundation of production to afford initial upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire Strength Upgrades">
    Invest your Oof into upgrades that enhance your Noob production and overall strength. Continue this until the next upgrades become too costly for quick acquisition.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Integrate Rune Progression">
    Once your basic Oof and upgrade loop is stable, begin rolling and leveling runes. Ensure you have activated any available Rune Speed and Rune Luck bonuses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Check Prestige Option">
    Monitor the in-game indicators for when a new prestige layer becomes available. Don't reset purely because progress is slow; evaluate the value of the next unlock.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Prestige and Rebuild">
    Execute the prestige, then restart your progression by rebuilding your Noob base, Oof generation, and upgrades. The new layer should provide enhanced scaling.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Repeat Through New Layers">
    Each successful prestige should allow you to reach new heights and unlock further content. The game is designed for repeated resets to drive progression.
  </Step>
</Steps>
