<Section icon={Target} title="Core Progression Loop: Oof Farming and Upgrades">
The heart of Noob Incremental lies in its core progression loop. You'll constantly be buying Noobs, earning Oof, and investing that Oof into various upgrades. Understanding this cycle is key to advancing efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Buy Noobs">
    Your journey starts by purchasing Noobs. Each Noob contributes to your Oof generation rate. The more Noobs you have, the faster you'll accumulate Oof.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Generate Oof">
    Oof is the fundamental resource. It's automatically generated by your Noobs over time. Keep an eye on your Oof per second (OPS) to gauge your production efficiency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Purchase Upgrades">
    Spend your accumulated Oof on upgrades available in the Shop. Upgrades come in various forms, boosting Noob efficiency, Oof production multipliers, and unlocking new features.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Roll and Level Runes">
    Once your basic Oof production is stable, introduce Rune rolling. Runes offer unique bonuses and can be leveled up to enhance their effects, adding a deeper layer of power.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Prestige for New Content">
    When your progress slows down, consider Prestiging. This resets some of your progress but unlocks new content, stronger bonuses, and allows you to scale even further.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Early Prestige">
Do not prestige too early. Ensure you have maximized your current layer's potential and are hitting a significant slowdown before resetting. Premature prestige can hinder your overall progress.
</Callout>
