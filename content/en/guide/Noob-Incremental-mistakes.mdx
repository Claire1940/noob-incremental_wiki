<Section icon={Trophy} title="Prestige Timing and Layer Mistakes">

The Prestige system is designed to break through "walls" where progress slows to a crawl. However, a common **Noob Incremental mistakes** trend is prestiging as soon as the button becomes available. While it is tempting to see new content, resetting too early can actually slow down your overall progression if you haven't maximized your current layer's potential.

You should only prestige when the time it takes to buy the next meaningful upgrade exceeds the time it would take to rebuild your current progress after a reset. This "Time-to-Value" calculation is the secret to top-tier placement on the leaderboards.

| Prestige Status | Action Recommended | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Early Access** | Wait | You haven't exhausted easy multipliers |
| **Stagnant Oof** | Prepare | Growth has slowed; check for last Runes |
| **Wall Reached** | Prestige | Upgrades are too expensive to reach |
| **Post-Reset** | Focus Noobs | Rapidly rebuild your Oof production base |

<Callout type="info" title="Prestige Knowledge">
Each prestige layer unlocks new mechanics. Ensure you have read the requirements for the next layer before resetting, as some layers require specific stat milestones rather than just Oof totals.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Scaling and Stat Errors">

As you move into multiple prestige layers, the complexity of Noob Incremental increases. Players often focus too much on Oof while ignoring secondary stats like Prism gain or Rune leveling. Prism is a vital long-term resource that supports account-wide bonuses, and neglecting it in favor of short-term Oof gains is a significant error.

Furthermore, many players fail to utilize Potions correctly. Using a Rune Luck potion during a period where you cannot afford many rolls is a waste of a rare resource. Potions should be saved for "burst" sessions where you have a large surplus of Tokens or Tickets.

| Stat Name | Primary Function | Scaling Type | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Oof Gain** | Early progression | Multiplicative | High |
| **Prism Gain** | Long-term account power | Flat/Bonus | Medium |
| **Rune Speed** | Rolling efficiency | Percentage | High |
| **Rune Luck** | Drop quality | Percentage | Very High |
| **Stat Multi** | General power | Layered | Medium |

<Callout type="warning" title="Potion Management">
Only activate Bulk Rune Potions or Luck Potions when you are prepared to stay active for the entire duration of the buff. Closing the game or idling without resources wastes the Potion's potential.
</Callout>
