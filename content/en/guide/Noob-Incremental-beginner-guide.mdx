<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Mastering the Rune System">

Once you have established a steady flow of Oof, the Rune system becomes your next major focus. Runes are unique items that can be rolled and leveled up to provide massive, permanent bonuses to your stats. This system is heavily influenced by your "Rune Luck" and "Rune Speed," both of which can be enhanced through community engagement.

The Rune system is designed to be a long-term progression mechanic. You roll for runes using specific resources, and each rune has a tier. Higher-tier runes provide better stat boosts, but they are significantly harder to find. This is where community bonuses become essential for any serious player.

| Bonus Type | Source | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rune Speed** | **Group + Like** | **+10% Rune Speed** for faster rolling sessions. |
| **Rune Luck** | **Discord** | **+10% Rune Luck** for better tier outcomes. |
| **Oof Bonus** | **Roblox Plus** | **+10% Oof** generation while active. |
| **Prism Bonus** | **Roblox Plus** | **+10% Prism** gain for late-game scaling. |

<Callout type="info" title="Community Setup">
To maximize your efficiency, ensure you have joined the Ghoulax Games Roblox group and liked the game. Then, link your Discord account in the community server. These actions are free and provide a permanent 20% total efficiency boost to your Rune progression.
</Callout>

Leveling your runes is just as important as finding them. As you collect duplicate runes or specific leveling materials, you can upgrade your existing runes to increase their potency. This ensures that even "common" runes remain useful as you transition into the mid-game.

| Rune Action | Player Value | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rolling** | Discover new stat boosts | When early upgrades feel too expensive. |
| **Leveling** | Increase current bonuses | To push past a specific prestige requirement. |
| **Equipping** | Activate stat changes | Always keep your highest-tier runes active. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Luck Potions">
If you have obtained Rune Luck Potions from codes, do not use them immediately. Save them for "Push Sessions" where you have at least 15-20 minutes of uninterrupted time to roll as many runes as possible.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Navigating Prestige Layers">

Prestige is the mechanic that allows you to reset your current progress in exchange for powerful, permanent multipliers and access to new content. In Noob Incremental, there are multiple prestige layers, each offering a deeper level of complexity. Understanding when to prestige is the difference between a top-tier player and a "noob."

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build the Base Run">
    Focus entirely on Noobs and Oof production. Do not worry about runes or prestige until your upgrades start costing millions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Buy Strength Upgrades">
    Exhaust the available shop upgrades. Once the "Next Upgrade" price is 100x your current OPS, you have reached a soft cap.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Add Rune Progression">
    Spend a few minutes rolling runes to squeeze out an extra 20-50% power. This often provides the final push needed for the prestige button to appear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate Rune Bonuses">
    Double-check that your Group and Discord bonuses are active. This ensures your post-prestige rune rolling is as efficient as possible.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Check the Prestige Option">
    Look at the prestige rewards. If the reset offers a "New Content Unlock" or at least a 5x multiplier to your overall growth, it is time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Prestige and Rebuild">
    Hit the button. You will lose your Oof and Noobs but keep your Runes and permanent Upgrades. Start buying Noobs immediately to get back to your previous speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="7" title="Repeat Through New Layers">
    Each prestige layer introduces new stats. Pay attention to "Prism" and other late-game resources that start appearing after your second or third reset.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Early Prestige Risk">
Reseting too early is a common mistake. If the prestige reward only offers a 1.2x or 1.5x multiplier, you will likely spend more time rebuilding than you saved. Aim for a multiplier that significantly changes how fast you can buy early upgrades.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Maximizing Free Rewards and Codes">

Codes are a vital part of the **Noob Incremental beginner guide** strategy. They provide "catch-up" mechanics that allow new players to skip the slowest parts of the initial grind. Most codes provide a mix of Tickets, Tokens, and Potions.
