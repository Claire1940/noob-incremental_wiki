<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Oof Farming and Upgrades">
Efficient Oof farming is the backbone of all progression in Noob Incremental. Without a consistent and growing Oof income, advancing through upgrades and later game systems becomes significantly slower. Understanding which upgrades to prioritize and when to invest is key.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Noob Investment">
    Begin by buying as many Noobs as your starting Oof allows. Noobs are your primary Oof generators.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Early Upgrade Focus">
    Spend your initial Oof on upgrades that directly boost Oof production or Noob efficiency. Look for upgrades with high multiplier values.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Code Redemption Timing">
    Redeem any active codes. The rewards (Tickets, Tokens, Potions) can provide a substantial early-game boost, especially Potions that increase Oof gain temporarily.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Balanced Growth">
    Continuously balance buying more Noobs with purchasing upgrades. When Oof income feels stagnant, check for new Noob tiers or powerful upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Community Boosts Integration">
    Ensure you've joined the Ghoulax Games group, liked the game, and joined the Ghoulax Studio Discord server to activate all passive Oof, Rune Speed, and Rune Luck bonuses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Smart Spending">
Always check the cost-to-benefit ratio of upgrades. Sometimes a slightly more expensive upgrade can provide a disproportionately larger boost to your Oof income, accelerating your progress significantly.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Strategic Prestige and Rebuilding">
Prestige is the ultimate reset mechanism in Noob Incremental, designed to unlock new content and push your progression further. Knowing when to prestige and how to rebuild efficiently after a reset is vital for continuous growth.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Assess Current Progress">
    Evaluate your current Oof income and upgrade costs. If progress has significantly slowed and new upgrades are prohibitively expensive, it might be time to consider prestige.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prepare for Prestige">
    Maximize any last-minute gains. Use any remaining potions, claim codes, and ensure your best runes are leveled to carry over maximum benefit into the next layer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Prestige">
    Access the in-game prestige option. Confirm the reset, understanding that you will lose most current progress but gain access to new content or a stronger starting point.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Efficient Rebuild">
    Post-prestige, return to the fundamental loop: buy Noobs, generate Oof, and invest in upgrades. Your previous prestige bonuses should make this initial phase much faster.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Explore New Content">
    With each prestige, new content layers or stronger buffs become available. Integrate these new elements into your strategy to accelerate future runs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Prestige Philosophy">
Prestige is not a failure of your current run, but a strategic step forward. Each prestige should empower your next run, allowing you to reach new milestones and unlock deeper game mechanics.
</Callout>
