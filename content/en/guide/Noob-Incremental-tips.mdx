### Prestige Cycle
Prestige is designed to reset your progress while unlocking new content and providing significant long-term power boosts.

<Section icon={Target} title="Noob Incremental Tips: Advanced Strategies and Common Mistakes">
Beyond the basics, a few advanced **Noob Incremental tips** can further streamline your progression. Avoiding common pitfalls will save you time and resources, allowing for a smoother journey through the game's many layers.

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Early Prestige Traps">
Prestiging too early, before you've maximized your current layer's gains or have a clear path to new content, can be detrimental. Always ensure you've hit a genuine progression wall or unlocked a significant new layer before resetting.

Consider these strategies for more efficient play:

- **Balanced Resource Allocation**: While Oof is crucial, don't neglect other resources or systems once they become available. A balanced approach to upgrades, runes, and other features will lead to more sustainable growth.
- **AFK Optimization**: As an incremental game, Noob Incremental benefits greatly from AFK (away from keyboard) time. Optimize your setup for passive Oof generation and resource accumulation before stepping away.
- **Experiment with Upgrades**: Don't just stick to one upgrade path. Experiment with different upgrade combinations to see which ones provide the best scaling for your current stage of progression.
