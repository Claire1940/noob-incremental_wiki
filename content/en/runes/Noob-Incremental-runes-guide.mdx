Rolling for runes is a central mechanic in Noob Incremental. You access this through the dedicated Rune menu, where you can use various currencies like Tokens or Tickets to obtain new power-ups. The process is governed by RNG (Random Number Generation), but it can be heavily influenced by your active potions and community-linked bonuses. Once a rune is obtained, it isn't static; you must actively level it to keep pace with the increasing costs of higher-tier Noob upgrades.

Leveling requires a specific resource management strategy. Early on, you might be tempted to level every rune you find, but the **Noob Incremental runes guide** recommends focusing on Oof multipliers and Prism gain initially. As you reach later prestige layers, you will shift your focus toward Rune Speed and Luck to make future rolling sessions more productive.
