<Section icon={Trophy} title="Advanced Leveling Strategies">

Advanced players focus on "Bulk Rolling" and "Target Leveling" to optimize their **Noob Incremental rune level** without wasting resources. As you progress, the cost of leveling becomes exponential, requiring a more calculated approach to resource management.

1.  **Bulk Rolling**: Save your tokens until you have enough for 50+ rolls. This increases the statistical likelihood of benefitting from your +10% Luck bonus.
2.  **Target Leveling**: Don't level every rune you find. Focus exclusively on runes that boost Oof Gain or Rune Speed until you reach the third prestige layer.
3.  **Potion Timing**: Only use Rune Luck potions when you have a large stockpile of currency. Using a potion for only 5-10 rolls is a waste of a rare resource.
