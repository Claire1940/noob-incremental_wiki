Prestige is the "reset" mechanic common in incremental games. In Noob Incremental, there are multiple prestige layers. Each layer resets your current Oof and Noobs but grants you a permanent currency or unlock that makes the next run significantly faster. The community consensus is to prestige as soon as the next layer's benefits outweigh the time it would take to continue the current run.

Do not ignore common runes. Even if you have the Discord luck bonus active, you will still roll many commons. Use these to "feed" and level up your rare runes. Leveling a single high-tier rune is often better than having a full set of level 1 mid-tier runes.
</Callout>

The prestige layers are where the game truly opens up. Each layer introduces new stats and mechanics that weren't visible in the early game. By staying active in the Discord, you can stay ahead of the "meta" and know exactly which stats to prioritize in the new layers, ensuring your progression remains efficient and your "walls" are kept to a minimum.
