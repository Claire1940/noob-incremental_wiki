### Prestige Strategy

Prestige is not just a reset; it's a strategic move to unlock new content and stronger progression layers. Knowing when to prestige and how to rebuild efficiently is vital.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build the Base Run">
    Focus on buying Noobs and generating Oof to establish initial production. Keep building while Oof gain and upgrade purchases feel fast.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Buy Strength Upgrades">
    Invest in upgrades that make your run stronger. Continue upgrading until the next useful upgrades take noticeably longer to reach.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Add Rune Progression">
    Roll and level up runes before pushing deeper into the run. Use rune systems once early Oof and upgrade growth are stable.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Check the Prestige Option">
    Open the in-game prestige layer when it becomes available. Do not reset only because progress slowed slightly; reset when the next prestige unlock gives better long-term value.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Prestige and Rebuild">
    Use prestige, then rebuild through Noobs, Oof generation, upgrades, and runes again. The reset creates a stronger long-term route through newly unlocked content.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Repeat Through New Layers">
    Use each stronger run to reach the next prestige layer and new content unlocks. The game is designed around multiple prestige layers, so repeated resets are part of normal progression.
  </Step>
</Steps>
