<Section icon={Map} title="Mastering the Core Farming Loop">

Effective **Noob Incremental farming** starts with understanding the primary resource: Oof. In this Roblox experience developed by Ghoulax Games, your progression is measured by how efficiently you can turn a single Noob into a massive Oof-generating empire. The fundamental cycle is simple: purchase Noobs to increase your base production, then use that Oof to buy upgrades that multiply your output.

However, the "incremental" nature of the game means that flat additions quickly fall off in value. To maintain a high farming speed, you must balance your spending between "Quantity" (more Noobs) and "Quality" (stronger upgrades). Early on, your focus should be entirely on Oof, as it unlocks the subsequent layers of the game, including Runes and Prestige.

| Resource | Primary Source | Farming Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Oof** | Noobs / Upgrades | Buying basic units and early-game scaling |
| **Prism** | Prestige / Bonuses | Advanced multipliers and mid-game unlocks |
| **Tickets** | Codes / Milestones | Purchasing temporary boosts and special items |
| **Tokens** | Codes / Events | Rare currency for permanent account upgrades |
| **Runes** | Rolling System | Long-term stat stacking and specialized buffs |

<Callout type="tip" title="Farming Efficiency">
Always prioritize upgrades that offer a "Multiplicative" bonus over "Additive" ones. As your Noob count increases, a 2x multiplier becomes exponentially more valuable than adding a flat +10 Oof per second.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Upgrade Priorities and Scaling Strategies">

With hundreds of upgrades available, it is easy for new players to become overwhelmed. To optimize your **Noob Incremental farming** route, you should categorize upgrades into three tiers: Production, Efficiency, and Utility. Production upgrades directly increase Oof per second, Efficiency upgrades reduce the cost of further purchases, and Utility upgrades improve secondary systems like Rune Speed or Luck.

In the early stages, "Production" is king. You need to hit specific Oof milestones to unlock the first prestige layer. Once you reach the mid-game, "Efficiency" becomes more important because the cost of Noobs will start to scale faster than your raw income.

<Section icon={Target} title="Rune System and Luck Optimization">

The Rune system is where **Noob Incremental farming** transitions from a simple clicker to a deep strategy game. Runes provide permanent or semi-permanent bonuses that persist through certain prestige layers. You roll for runes using the in-game interface, and their effectiveness is determined by both the level of the rune and your global Luck stat.

To maximize your rune efficiency, you must take advantage of community-based bonuses. Ghoulax Games offers specific incentives for engaging with their social platforms. Joining the official Roblox group and liking the game provides a +10% Rune Speed bonus, while joining the community Discord server provides a +10% Rune Luck bonus. Stacking these is mandatory for high-level play.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Prestige Strategy and Layer Progression">

Prestige is the ultimate tool in **Noob Incremental farming**. By resetting your current progress, you earn Prism, which is used to unlock permanent upgrades that make your next run significantly faster. The game features multiple prestige layers, each offering deeper content and stronger scaling.

Knowing when to prestige is an art. If you prestige the moment the button appears, you might not earn enough Prism to buy a meaningful upgrade. If you wait too long, your Oof production will hit a "wall" where the next upgrade is hours away, wasting time that could be spent in a faster, post-prestige run.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build the Foundation">
    Focus entirely on Oof and Noobs. Do not worry about runes or secondary stats until your Oof production is stable and upgrades feel expensive.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Optimize with Runes">
    Once progression slows, start rolling runes. Use any available Potions (from codes) to increase your Luck during this phase to snag a high-tier multiplier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Push to Prestige">
    Continue buying upgrades until the time it takes to reach the next "10x" multiplier exceeds 15 minutes. This is usually the optimal time to look at the prestige menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute and Rebuild">
    Prestige to earn Prism. Immediately spend that Prism on "Base Oof" or "Cost Reduction" upgrades to make your next trip to the prestige button 50% faster.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Prestige Layer | Unlock Requirement | Main Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Layer 1** | Oof Milestone | Prism and basic permanent upgrades |
| **Layer 2** | Prism Milestone | Advanced stats and secondary rune slots |
| **Layer 3** | Secret Requirement | Specialized content and massive multipliers |

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Early Resets">
Your first prestige is the hardest. Resist the urge to reset for a tiny amount of Prism. Wait until you can afford at least two or three permanent upgrades in the Prism shop to ensure your second run isn't just a repeat of the first.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Maximizing Boosts and Daily Tasks">

To keep your **Noob Incremental farming** at peak performance, you need to manage your temporary boosts and daily interactions. Potions are consumable items that provide massive, short-term buffs to Oof gain, Rune Luck, or Rune Speed. These should be saved for "Push Sessions" where you are actively playing and can maximize every second of the boost.

Additionally, the game rewards players who are part of the Roblox ecosystem. If you have Roblox Plus, you receive an automatic +10% Oof and +10% Prism bonus. While not required, these small percentages stack with your upgrades and runes, leading to a much higher "ceiling" for your production.
