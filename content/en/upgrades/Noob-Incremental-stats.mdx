<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimizing Your Stats: A Step-by-Step Approach">
Strategic stat optimization is crucial for efficient progression in Noob Incremental. Follow these steps to ensure you're always getting the most out of your game time and resources.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Oof & Upgrade Focus">
    At the start of any run, your primary goal is to establish a strong Oof production base. Invest heavily in Noobs and early upgrades that directly boost Oof generation. This initial push will provide the resources needed for more advanced stat investments.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock & Enhance Core Upgrades">
    As Oof income grows, transition to purchasing upgrades that offer more significant stat multipliers or unlock new stat categories. Pay attention to upgrades that reduce future costs or increase overall efficiency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Integrate Rune Progression">
    Once your basic Oof and upgrade loops are stable, begin rolling and leveling runes. Runes introduce powerful, often percentage-based, stat bonuses that are vital for mid to late-game scaling.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Leverage Community Bonuses">
    Before any major rune rolling sessions or long upgrade pushes, ensure you've claimed all available community bonuses. Joining the Ghoulax Games group and liking the game provides +10% Rune Speed, while joining the community Discord offers +10% Rune Luck. These are free stat boosts that significantly impact efficiency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Strategic Prestige">
    When progression significantly slows down, and the next major upgrade or rune seems out of reach, it's time to consider prestiging. Prestige resets your progress but unlocks new content, stronger base stats, and higher multipliers, allowing you to achieve even greater stat levels in subsequent runs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Premature Prestige">
Do not prestige solely because progress has slowed slightly. Only prestige when the game explicitly offers it, and the next prestige layer promises substantial long-term benefits and unlocks new content. Early prestige can hinder your overall progression if not timed correctly.
</Callout>
