<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Understanding Noob Incremental Upgrades">
Noob Incremental is all about scaling your power through various systems, with upgrades being a cornerstone of this progression. The game features hundreds of upgrades designed to boost your Oof production, enhance your Noobs, and prepare you for prestiging. Understanding which upgrades to prioritize can significantly impact your early game speed and overall efficiency.

The core gameplay loop involves buying Noobs to generate Oof, then using that Oof to purchase upgrades. These upgrades, in turn, make your Noobs more efficient, leading to even greater Oof income. This cycle is crucial for advancing through the game's multiple prestige layers and unlocking new content.
