<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Upgrade Strategies and Prestige Preparation">

The late game in Noob Incremental revolves around optimizing your upgrade path for efficient Prestige. Prestige resets your progress but unlocks new content and stronger multipliers, making each subsequent run more powerful. Your upgrade choices should reflect this goal, aiming for maximum power before a reset.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Maximize Current Layer Power">
    Before considering Prestige, invest in the most impactful upgrades available in your current layer. This includes high-tier Noobs, powerful Oof multipliers, and stat-boosting upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Optimize Rune Loadout">
    Ensure your active runes are leveled and provide optimal bonuses for your current build. Use Rune Luck bonuses if you're hunting for specific, powerful runes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Claim All Bonuses">
    Double-check that you have activated all available bonuses, including Roblox Plus (+10% Oof, +10% Prism), Group/Like bonuses (+10% Rune Speed), and Community Server bonuses (+10% Rune Luck).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Evaluate Prestige Gains">
    When the option to Prestige becomes available, carefully weigh the benefits of resetting against continuing your current run. Prestige when the new content or multipliers offer a clear path to faster future progression.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Prestige Caution">
Do not rush into Prestige. While it's a critical mechanic, performing it too early without sufficient power can leave you struggling in the next layer. Ensure you've hit a reasonable wall in your current layer first.
</Callout>
