<Section icon={Target} title="Strategic Prestige Steps to Max World">

Prestige is the mechanism that allows you to break through progression walls and unlock new content, moving you closer to the **Noob Incremental max world**. Each prestige offers a fresh start with new benefits and challenges, making strategic timing vital.

<Callout type="info">
The official game description emphasizes "multiple prestige layers" as the path to unlock stronger progression. Embrace the reset as a growth opportunity.
</Callout>

The process of prestiging involves resetting your current progress in exchange for a permanent bonus or unlock. This allows you to start a new run with increased power, enabling you to reach further than before.

**Prestige Progression Steps:**

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Your Base Run">
    Focus on maximizing Oof generation and acquiring essential upgrades. Ensure your core production loop is robust before considering a reset.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Integrate Rune Power">
    Once Oof and upgrades are flowing, start rolling and leveling runes. Activate community bonuses for Rune Speed and Luck to enhance this process.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reach a Soft Cap">
    Continue pushing until your progress significantly slows, and the next meaningful upgrades or Oof gains become excessively expensive. This is your signal for prestige.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Check Prestige Benefits">
    Before confirming the reset, review the specific rewards and unlocks offered by the next prestige layer. Ensure these benefits outweigh the temporary loss of your current progress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Prestige and Rebuild Stronger">
    Execute the prestige. Your goal in the new run is to leverage the permanent bonuses to reach your previous soft cap much faster and push beyond it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Repeat and Conquer New Layers">
    Each successful prestige will open up access to new content, stats, and upgrades. Continuously refine your strategy with each reset to climb towards the Noob Incremental max world.
  </Step>
</Steps>
