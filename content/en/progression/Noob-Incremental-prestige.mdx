<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding the Noob Incremental Prestige System">

The **Noob Incremental prestige** system serves as the backbone of long-term progression in this Roblox idle experience. Unlike basic upgrades that only provide additive or small multiplicative bonuses to your current run, prestiging resets your Noobs and Oof count to zero while granting access to deeper layers of the game. This reset is necessary because Oof production eventually hits a "wall" where the cost of the next upgrade exceeds your realistic earning potential.

When you trigger a prestige, you aren't just starting over; you are evolving your account. Each prestige layer is designed to introduce a new resource or mechanic that makes reaching your previous peak significantly faster. For example, early layers focus on Oof multipliers, while later layers integrate Rune rolling and Prism bonuses.

| Progression Type | Reset on Prestige | Permanent Benefit | Primary Resource |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Noob Count** | Yes | Higher starting efficiency | Oof |
| **Oof Upgrades** | Yes | Unlocks new upgrade tiers | Oof |
| **Rune Levels** | No | Persistent stat boosts | Tokens / Tickets |
| **Prestige Layers** | No | Access to new gameplay | Prestige Points |
| **Community Boosts** | No | Constant % multipliers | Social Actions |

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficiency Tip">
Before hitting the prestige button, check if you have any unspent Oof that could buy one last permanent stat upgrade. While Oof resets, the stats gained from specific milestone upgrades often carry over or influence the strength of your next prestige reward.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Rune Integration and Prestige Synergy">

Runes are a secondary progression system that becomes vital after your first few **Noob Incremental prestige** cycles. Unlike Oof, Runes and their levels persist through resets. This makes them the most valuable long-term investment in the game.

The synergy between prestige and runes is simple: Prestiging gives you the resources (or the multiplier) to earn Tokens faster, which you then spend on rolling Runes. Those Runes provide permanent stat boosts that make your next prestige run even faster.

| Rune Type | Prestige Benefit | Bonus Source | Required Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Speed Rune** | Faster Oof ticks | Group Membership | Join Ghoulax Games |
| **Luck Rune** | Better Rune rolls | Discord Server | Join Community Discord |
| **Oof Rune** | Massive Oof % | Roblox Plus | Subscribe to Premium |
| **Prism Rune** | End-game scaling | Milestone Reward | Reach Layer 3 |

To optimize this loop, you should focus on "Rune Luck." The higher your luck, the better the Runes you roll. Better Runes mean you can hit your prestige requirements in minutes rather than hours.

</Callout>
