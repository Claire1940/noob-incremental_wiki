<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Runes and Prestige Layers">
Runes and Prestige are the two key systems that allow you to break through progression walls in Noob Incremental World 3. Runes offer incremental boosts and special abilities, while Prestige resets your progress but unlocks new content and significantly stronger base stats for your next run.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Rune Rolling">
    Begin rolling runes once your basic Oof and upgrade growth have stabilized. Runes provide a deeper layer of scaling beyond simple upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Activate Rune Bonuses">
    Join the Ghoulax Games group and like the Roblox experience for **+10% Rune Speed**. Also, join the community server for **+10% Rune Luck**. These bonuses are crucial for effective rune farming.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Level Up Key Runes">
    Prioritize leveling up runes that directly benefit your Oof production, upgrade efficiency, or Prestige gains. Focus on quality over quantity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Strategic Prestige Timing">
    Prestige when your current progression slows significantly, and the next Prestige layer offers substantial new content or permanent bonuses. Don't prestige too early, as it can hinder your overall growth.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Efficient Rebuilding Post-Prestige">
    After prestiging, quickly rebuild your Oof production and repurchase essential upgrades. Leverage the new Prestige bonuses to reach your previous milestones faster and push further.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Prestige Caution">
Avoid prestiging solely because progress has slowed slightly. Only reset when the next Prestige unlock provides a clear, long-term advantage that outweighs the temporary setback of a reset.
</Callout>

</Section>
