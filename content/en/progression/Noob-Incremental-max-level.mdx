<Section icon={Trophy} title="Understanding Max Level in Noob Incremental">
Noob Incremental, like many Roblox idle games, doesn't feature a traditional "max level" in the way players might expect from an RPG. Instead, progression is structured around **multiple prestige layers**. Each prestige acts as a soft reset, allowing players to unlock new content, gain significant power boosts, and continue scaling their Oof production and overall strength. The core loop involves buying Noobs, generating Oof, acquiring upgrades, rolling and leveling Runes, and then Prestiging to advance.

<Callout type="tip" title="Progression Mindset">
Think of "max level" not as a cap, but as a series of gates that open to deeper, more complex, and more rewarding gameplay loops. The goal is to efficiently reach and clear each prestige layer.
</Callout>
