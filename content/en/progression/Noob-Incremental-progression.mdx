### Core Progression Loop Overview

The game's progression is built on a cyclical system designed to constantly push you towards higher numbers and new unlocks.

| System      | Player Action                       | Effect                                          | Checkpoint                                                                |
| :---------- | :---------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Noobs**   | Buy Noobs as the core starting action. | Noobs help generate Oof, the main early progression output. | Start every run by improving Noob-based production before chasing later systems. |
| **Oof**     | Generate Oof through Noobs and strengthen it with upgrades. | Oof is the main production target. Roblox Plus players receive +10% Oof. | Prioritize Oof growth when upgrades are still affordable and gains are moving quickly. |
| **Upgrades**| Buy upgrades after building enough Oof production. | Upgrades make the player stronger and support faster scaling. | Keep upgrading while costs feel reachable; when progress slows, check runes and prestige options. |
| **Runes**   | Roll runes and level them up during progression. | Runes are part of the official power loop and help deepen scaling. | Add rune rolling after basic Oof and upgrade growth are already moving. |
| **Prestige**| Prestige after building enough power from Noobs, upgrades, stats, and runes. | Prestige unlocks new content and adds deeper progression layers. | Prestige when the in-game option is available and the next unlock is more valuable than staying in the current run. |

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Prestige and Unlocking New Content">

Prestige is the ultimate reset mechanism in Noob Incremental, allowing you to unlock new content and reach deeper layers of **Noob Incremental progression**. Knowing when and how to prestige efficiently is key to long-term success.

<Callout type="info" title="Prestige Goal">
Prestige should be viewed as a strategic reset that enables significantly faster progress in subsequent runs, rather than just a simple reset. It's about unlocking new game mechanics and multipliers.
</Callout>

### Prestige Strategy Steps

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build a Strong Base Run">
    Focus on maximizing Oof production and acquiring essential upgrades. This foundational strength will make your prestige reset more impactful.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Optimize Runes">
    Before considering prestige, ensure your runes are rolled and leveled to a satisfactory point. Strong runes carry over and provide a significant boost to your next run.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate All Bonuses">
    Double-check that all available community and game bonuses (Rune Speed, Rune Luck, Oof, Prism) are active. These amplify your gains leading up to and immediately after prestige.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Identify the Plateau">
    Prestige when your current progression significantly slows down, and the cost of the next meaningful upgrade or unlock becomes disproportionately high. This indicates it's time for a reset.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Initiate Prestige">
    Access the in-game prestige option. Confirm your decision, understanding that most of your current progress will reset, but new content and permanent buffs will be unlocked.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Rebuild and Scale">
    After prestiging, return to the basic loop of buying Noobs, generating Oof, and acquiring upgrades. You will notice significantly faster progress due to the permanent buffs from prestige.
  </Step>
  <Step num="7" title="Repeat and Conquer New Layers">
    The game is designed around multiple prestige layers. Continuously repeat the cycle, using each prestige to push further and unlock even more content and power.
  </Step>
</Steps>
